Platform Features

Everything in one platform

POS Terminal — Live

Lightning-fast tap-to-order interface.

Designed for high-volume cafes, our POS is optimized for speed and accuracy. It features a live order cart, category filtering, and instant customer lookup.

  • AI Cross-sells: Smart "You may also like" suggestions
  • Live Sync: Instantly pushes orders to KDS via WebSocket
  • Instant Payments: Integrated Cash, Card, and UPI

Kitchen Display System

A full-screen, real-time order board.

Orders appear instantly via WebSocket push from the POS. Kitchen staff can tap any item to toggle its state—no page refresh required.

  • Color-coded urgency: Keep track of long-pending orders
  • Status updates: New → Cooking → Ready → Completed
  • Device agnostics: Works on any tablet, monitor, or TV

Floor & Tables

Interactive canvas to map seating.

Easily design your floor plan using our drag-and-drop grid canvas. See which tables are Free, Occupied, or Reserved in real-time.

  • Live Status: Syncs instantly with the POS
  • Table Assignment: Assign orders directly to a table
  • Admin Overrides: Force-unlock any table if needed

Products & Catalog

Manage your entire menu with ease.

Add new items, set prices, upload photos, and organize categories all in one place. Group products for fast POS navigation.

  • Custom Tags: Mark as Chef's Special, Vegan, or New
  • Featured Flags: Add a visual badge to top sellers
  • Smart Linking: Configure AI cross-sell recommendations

Loyalty Program

Build lasting customer relationships.

Turn first-time visitors into regulars by creating customer profiles with complete visit history and automated point tracking.

  • Auto-tracking: Award points per order automatically
  • Easy Redemption: Customers redeem perks right at checkout
  • Customer Lookup: Search by name or phone at the POS

Feedback & Reports

Automated smart feedback collection.

Automatically trigger review emails after an order is completed. Track ratings and comments in a live reporting dashboard.

  • Email Automation: Sent with unique signed tokens
  • Live Feed: View all ratings and comments instantly
  • Staff Metrics: Performance linked to customer satisfaction

Coupons & Promos

Drive sales with flexible discounts.

Create fixed-amount or percentage-based discounts to reward customers and boost off-peak revenue.

  • Time-limited: Set precise start and end datetimes
  • Happy Hours: Unlimited or restricted usage
  • POS Integration: Apply instantly from a POS dropdown

Branded Receipts

Customize receipts for your brand.

Provide a premium post-purchase experience by designing digital and printed receipts that match your cafe's aesthetic.

  • Custom Branding: Upload your cafe logo
  • Personalized Messages: Custom header and footer text
  • Feedback QR: Embedded code linking to your review page

AI Chat Assistant

Your 24/7 intelligent cafe consultant.

An embedded AI-powered chatbot that knows your live menu. It scrapes your product data to provide highly accurate assistance.

  • Live Context: AI understands your current active menu
  • Rich Product Cards: Generates interactive UI bubbles
  • Business Strategy: Ask for dynamic cross-sell strategies

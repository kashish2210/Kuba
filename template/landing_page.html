Why Kuba exists

I am built for the super admin and for cafe owners who want a clean organisation workspace.

A super admin can create organisations manually, or a cafe owner can sign up and let Kuba create the organisation automatically. After login, the org admin lands in a dedicated dashboard for employees, products, floor plans, payment methods, coupons, sessions, and reports.

03

04
